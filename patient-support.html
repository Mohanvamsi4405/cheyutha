Patient Support

We are here to support you through your journey with kidney disease and organ transplant. We offer help every step of the way.

Our Services

Medical Assistance

We provide guidance on new treatments, offer subsidized medicines, and supply dialyzer kits to ensure patients receive the care they need without financial strain.

Emotional Support

Our network of experienced transplant recipients provides empathetic counseling and a strong support system, helping patients and their families build resilience and hope.

Financial Aid

We offer financial assistance to eligible patients to help cover the costs of transplant surgery, post-operative care, and other related medical expenses.

Transportation Services

To ease the burden of travel, we offer transportation support to ensure you can get to and from your medical appointments, including dialysis sessions and check-ups.
